Fortress Defense

Build impenetrable defenses, command powerful weapons, and protect your castle from relentless invaders!

Game Overview

Fortress Defense is a challenging medieval tower defense game where you are tasked with protecting your castle from waves of determined invaders. Build a variety of defensive structures including archer towers, cannon emplacements, and magical barriers to halt the enemy advance. Manage your resources wisely as you strengthen your defenses and upgrade your fortress to withstand increasingly powerful sieges.

How to Play

Click on build icons to select defensive structures, then place them at strategic points along the enemy's path. Collect gold from defeated enemies to construct more towers or upgrade existing ones. Each tower type has different strengths and weaknesses—archers are effective against infantry, cannons excel against armored units, and magic towers can hit flying enemies. Monitor enemy waves and adapt your defense strategy accordingly.

Game Features

  • 8 different defensive tower types with unique attack patterns
  • 20 challenging levels with varied terrain and pathways
  • 3-tier upgrade system for each defensive structure
  • Multiple enemy types including infantry, cavalry, siege engines, and flying units
  • Special abilities and power-ups to overcome difficult waves

Strategy Tips

Focus on building a mix of tower types to handle different enemy units. Place slowing towers near the entrance to give your damage dealers more time to attack. Upgrade strategically – sometimes a few powerful towers are better than many weak ones. Pay attention to the terrain and use choke points to your advantage. Save special abilities for large waves or when boss units appear.

A true defender doesn't rely on the strength of walls alone, but on the strategic placement of every archer, cannon, and spell!
