Hero Rush Tower Defense

Summon legendary heroes, build powerful defenses, and protect your realm from the invading hordes!

Game Overview

Hero Rush Tower Defense is an epic strategy game that combines classic tower defense with hero-based combat. Recruit a team of diverse heroes, each with unique abilities and strengths, to defend your kingdom from waves of monsters and enemies. Strategically place your heroes along the path to maximize their effectiveness and prevent the invaders from reaching your base.

How to Play

Click to select and place heroes at strategic points along the enemy path. Earn gold by defeating enemies and use it to summon more heroes or upgrade existing ones. Each hero has special abilities that can be activated when needed. Pay attention to enemy types and adjust your strategy accordingly – different heroes are effective against different enemies.

Game Features

  • 12 unique heroes with distinct abilities and attack styles
  • 30 challenging levels across varied fantasy environments
  • Progressive hero upgrade system with powerful enhancements
  • Diverse enemy types including ground troops, flying units, and boss monsters
  • Special power-ups and spells to turn the tide of difficult battles

Strategy Tips

Place ranged heroes on elevated positions for better coverage. Use heroes with area damage to handle groups of enemies. Prioritize upgrading a core team of heroes rather than spreading resources thinly. Save your special abilities for boss waves or when enemies are clustered together for maximum impact.

The key to victory isn't just having the strongest heroes, but placing them in the right positions at the right time!
