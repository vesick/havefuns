HeroTower

Deploy your heroes, build your defenses, and protect your kingdom from the invading hordes!

Game Overview

HeroTower is a strategic tower defense game where you command legendary heroes to protect your kingdom. Position your champions wisely, upgrade their abilities, and deploy special powers to halt the advancing enemy forces. Each level presents new challenges that will test your tactical prowess.

How to Play

Click on hero icons and place them on strategic positions on the map. Collect gold from defeated enemies to summon more heroes or upgrade existing ones. Each hero has unique abilities that can be activated with special power buttons. Plan your defenses carefully to prevent enemies from reaching your castle!

Game Features

  • 10 unique hero classes with special abilities
  • 25 challenging levels with increasing difficulty
  • Complex upgrade system for hero progression
  • Various enemy types with different strengths and weaknesses
  • Epic boss battles that require strategic planning

Strategy Tips

Position ranged heroes on high ground for increased attack range and damage. Place tank heroes at choke points to block enemy advancement. Focus on upgrading a few powerful heroes rather than deploying many weak ones. Save your special abilities for when large waves of enemies appear or during boss fights.

A well-positioned hero is worth more than a dozen scattered across the battlefield. Strategy trumps numbers!
