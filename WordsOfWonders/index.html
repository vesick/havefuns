Words of Wonders

Discover hidden words, solve relaxing puzzles, and explore beautiful landmarks around the world!

Game Overview

Words of Wonders is a captivating word puzzle game that combines gorgeous scenery with engaging vocabulary challenges. Journey through famous landmarks around the world, solving crossword-style puzzles and finding hidden words. As you progress, you'll rebuild these iconic landmarks while expanding your vocabulary and exercising your brain in a relaxing, stress-free environment.

How to Play

Swipe to connect letters on the wheel to form words that fit into the crossword puzzle. All words must be valid dictionary words. Complete the required words to advance to the next level. Look carefully at letter patterns and think of word possibilities based on the available letters. You can use hints if you get stuck, but try to save them for the more challenging levels.

Game Features

  • Hundreds of levels across famous global landmarks
  • Beautiful graphics and satisfying word-connection mechanics
  • Innovative letter wheel that creates varied puzzles
  • Bonus words for extra coins and achievements
  • Progressive difficulty that challenges vocabulary skills

Word-Finding Tips

Look for common prefixes and suffixes when searching for words. Pay attention to vowel patterns to help identify possible words. If you're stuck, try tracing different paths through the letters to see what words emerge. Sometimes finding longer words first makes it easier to spot the shorter ones. Don't forget to look for plural forms of words by adding 'S'.

The best word puzzlers don't just know many words—they see patterns in letters that others miss. Train your eye to recognize common combinations!
